# Competitor Analysis — ishitabrass.com

## Ishita Industries (Jamnagar) — Current #1 for "brass components"

### What They Have
- Product catalog with categories
- Basic contact form
- Export certifications mentioned
- Some product photography

### Their Gaps (Our Opportunities)
- No RFQ form — visitors can't request a quote inline
- No WhatsApp button — biggest missed mobile conversion
- No animations or scroll effects — feels static
- Generic typography — no brand identity
- No testimonials or social proof
- No industry vertical pages (plumbing, automotive, electrical, etc.)
- No stats/credibility numbers displayed
- Slow page load (unoptimized images)
- No mobile-optimized experience

### Our Differentiators
1. **WhatsApp-first CTA** — instant response expectation
2. **Inline RFQ form** — reduce friction to zero
3. **Industry verticals** — speak buyer's language
4. **Social proof** — testimonials + client logos
5. **Animation polish** — scroll reveal, counters, hero parallax
6. **Editorial typography** — Playfair Display or Fraunces serif
7. **Stats prominence** — 5000+ products, 700+ clients, 40 years
8. **Quality section** — ISO certifications, QC process

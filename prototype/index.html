About Us

Four Decades of Brass Engineering Excellence

Founded in 1985 in Jamnagar — the brass capital of India — Deepak Products has grown from a small workshop to a globally trusted manufacturer of precision brass components. Our state-of-the-art facility processes 500+ tonnes of brass annually.

Every component we manufacture undergoes rigorous quality checks, ensuring that our products meet international standards and exceed customer expectations — whether you're ordering 100 pieces or 1,000,000.
